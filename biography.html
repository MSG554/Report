Umoja King'ori Secondary School is the government day school established in 2018, found at King'ori Ward,in Meru district, Arusha.

It is about 25Kms from Kibaoni station, Main Arusha-Moshi Road to the school.

Currently, the school have 15 active workers, having 15 teachers and 2 non-teaching staff. under the strong leadership of Mr. Fred Paul Lubida.
